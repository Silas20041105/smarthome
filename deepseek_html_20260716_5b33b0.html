Reading & Grammar: Paragraph 1

"Imagine waking up in the morning. Your curtains open by themselves. The lights turn on softly. Your favorite music starts to play. This is not a dream — this is a smart home!"
想象一下在早晨醒来。你的窗帘自动拉开。灯光柔和地亮起。你最喜欢的音乐开始播放。这不是在做梦——这就是智能家居！

Key Vocab

by themselves: 自动地
softly: 柔和地

Grammar Focus

Imagine doing sth.: 想象做某事

Sentence Structure

This is not A — this is B!

Reading & Grammar: Paragraph 2

"A smart home uses technology to make life easier. You can control things with your voice or a phone. You can say, 'Turn on the lights,' and the lights will turn on. You can say, 'Play music,' and your speaker will play a song. You can even ask your fridge to order milk when you run out!"
智能家居利用技术让生活更轻松。你可以用声音或手机控制事物。你可以说："开灯"，灯就会亮起。你可以说："播放音乐"，音箱就会播放歌曲。当牛奶喝完时，你甚至可以叫冰箱订购牛奶！

Key Vocab

control: 控制
run out: 用完

Grammar Focus

Imperative sentences: Turn on / Play...

Sentence Structure

use sth. to do sth.

Reading & Grammar: Paragraph 3

"Smart homes are not just about fun. They can help save energy too. Smart thermostats can learn when you are home and when you are away. They turn down the heating when nobody is home. This saves electricity and helps the environment."
智能家居不仅仅是为了好玩。它们也能帮助节约能源。智能恒温器能学习你何时在家、何时离开。当无人在家时，它们会调低暖气。这能节约电力，也有助于保护环境。

Key Vocab

save energy: 节约能源
thermostat: 恒温器

Grammar Focus

Time clauses: when nobody is home

Sentence Structure

not just about...

Reading & Grammar: Paragraph 4

"In Shenzhen, many new buildings already have smart home technology. Some people use smart doorbells with cameras. They can see who is at the door from their phone, even when they are at work. Others use smart robots to clean the floor while they sleep."
在深圳，许多新建筑已经拥有了智能家居技术。一些人使用带摄像头的智能门铃。即使在上班，他们也能通过手机看到谁在门口。其他人则使用智能机器人趁他们睡觉时打扫地板。

Key Vocab

doorbell: 门铃
already: 已经

Grammar Focus

while 从句: while they sleep

Sentence Structure

Some..., others...

Reading & Grammar: Paragraph 5

"However, smart homes also have problems. They can be expensive to set up. Sometimes the internet stops working, and then the smart devices stop too. Some people worry about privacy - what if someone hacks into the system?"
然而，智能家居也有问题。它们的安装成本可能很高。有时网络停止工作，智能设备也会跟着停止。有些人担心隐私问题——如果有人黑进系统怎么办？

Key Vocab

expensive: 昂贵的
privacy: 隐私
hack into: 黑客入侵

Grammar Focus

what if...?

Sentence Structure

However, ... also have problems.

Reading & Grammar: Paragraph 6

"Technology is changing fast. Maybe one day, every home will be a smart home. What do you think? Would you like to live in a smart home?"
科技正在快速改变。也许有一天，每个家庭都会是智能家居。你认为呢？你愿意住在智能家居里吗？

Key Vocab

changing fast: 快速改变

Grammar Focus

would like to do

Sentence Structure

What do you think?
